## Other Midi channels mapping

Midi Channel 2 (from cc 0 to cc 127) will be mapped on BCR2000 presets 4 , 5 and 6 .

Midi Channel 3 (from cc 0 to cc 127) will be mapped on BCR2000 presets 7 , 8 and 9 ,
and so on .
